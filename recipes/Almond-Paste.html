Almond Paste

You've heard of paste, you've heard of almonds, from the makers of marzipan it's: I can't believe it's not marzipan! This recipe is essentially and americanized marzipan. I like it better than marzipan because I think rose water is a nice color but once you get into the whole baking scene it seems like more work than it's worth. Call me a sinner, if you can, I'll enjoy my marzipan. Also, still going to use rose water I guess.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups very finely ground blanched almond flour/meal
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tsps pure almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon quality food grade rose water
  • 1 egg white
